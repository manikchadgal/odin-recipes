Jalebi





Description

the jalebis are sweets having crispy, chewy texture with a crystallized sugary coating. bengalis usually enjoy these juicy jalebis hot in their daily breakfast. you can enjoy this hot or cold as a dessert. jalebis are popularly served during ramadan and diwali.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tsp corn flour
  • 0.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tsp yogurt
  • 10 tsp water
  • 0.25 tsp turmeric powder


Steps

  1. in a mixing bowl, mix maida, corn flour, curd. then add half tsp vinegar and water. mix well in round circular directions for 4 minutes.
  2. now add baking soda and give a gentle mix.
  3. now pour this batter in the tomato ketchup bottles carefully.
  4. heat oil in a pan and add 1 tbsp of ghee.
  5. squeeze the bottle and make round spirals with the batter.
  6. when one side is partly cooked, turn over and fry the other side.
  7. fry till the jalebis are a light golden. then immediately drop the fried jalebis in the
  8. warm sugar syrup for 30 seconds (1 string consistency).
  9. finally, serve jalebis hot, warm or at room temperature.
